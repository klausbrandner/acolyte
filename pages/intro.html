1. Download Acolyte

Download the Acolyte-Package from our website and extract it into your web-project.

2. Include Libraries and Frameworks

Since Acolyte uses jQuery and AngularJs, you need to include following libraries to your html files.
Just copy and paste these lines into the header-Tag of your html files.
<!-- jQuery & AngularJS Libraries --> <script src="//ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/2.0.0/jquery.min.js"></script> <script src="//ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jqueryui/1.11.4/jquery-ui.min.js"></script> <script src="//ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/angularjs/1.4.6/angular.min.js"></script> <script src="//ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/angularjs/1.4.6/angular-sanitize.js"></script> <!-- Acolyte Stylesheet & Javascript --> <link rel="stylesheet" href="acolyte/acolyte.css" /> <script src="acolyte/acolyte.min.js"></script>

3. Make your Website an Angular App

Simply make your website an Angular App in order to get things running. <html ng-app"Acolyte">

4. Include Acolyte Templates

There is one more thing. Include all our templates into your project. <aco-root></aco-root>

5. MySQL connection

Acolyte stores your content into a MySQL Database. You will find a settings.php file in your Acolyte folder. Please define the connection data.
